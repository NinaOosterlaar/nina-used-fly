# Day 9

- Arrive by plane at Busan Airport at 21.10 from [Jeju Island](/blog/plans/three-weeks-south-korea/jeju-island).
- Take the airport bus shuttle to the Air Sky Hotel.

# Day 10

- Go to LOTTE rent-a-car at Busan airport and pick up the car at 10.00.
- Drive to [Mount Gayasan](/blog/plans/three-weeks-south-korea/temple-stay-haeinsa) for our temple stay (1.5 hour drive).

# Day 12

- Arrive from either [Jeonju](/blog/plans/three-weeks-south-korea/jeonju) (2.5 hour drive) or [Boseong](/blog/plans/three-weeks-south-korea/boseong) (1.5 hour drive).
- Hand in the car at LOTTE rent-a-car before 21.00.
